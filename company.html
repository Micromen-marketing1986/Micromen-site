About Us

Company Overview

Micromen Software Solutions is a technology consulting and product company with over 40 years of expertise in delivering next-generation solutions for the construction and real-estate industry. Our commitment is simple yet powerful: to empower our customers with innovative, reliable, and future-ready technology that delivers measurable value.

Backed by deep domain knowledge and a strong team of experts, we specialize in industry-specific ERP solutions for construction and real estate, complemented by digital transformation services, application development, and strategic consulting. Every solution we build is crafted to meet real-world operational challenges and enhance productivity, visibility, and decision-making for construction businesses.

Our Journey

Company History

Micromen began its journey in 1986 as a partnership firm founded by a group of technocrats who were early pioneers in customized business software in India. In the early years, the company served multiple industries, providing tailored solutions for manufacturing, trading, finance, and construction companies.

In 1995, Micromen evolved into a Private Limited Company, marking a major shift in growth strategy and operational vision. With rapid expansion through channel partnerships and a sharpened focus on product innovation, the company established itself as a trusted technology partner across regions.

As the construction sector grew in scale and complexity, Micromen made a strategic decision to focus exclusively on this domain. This led to the creation of BuildSuperfast ERP, a comprehensive and deeply specialized solution designed to address the end-to-end needs of builders, developers, and contractors. The product quickly became the company’s flagship offering, widely recognized by clients as one of the most powerful and reliable construction & real-estate ERPs in the industry.

Where We Are

Our Presence

Today, Micromen has a strong Pan-Asian footprint, with offices in Chennai, Bangalore, and Mumbai, and channel partners in Delhi, Qatar, and Oman.

Our services division additionally operates from Chennai with an international office in Florida, USA, supporting global clients and enterprise implementations.
